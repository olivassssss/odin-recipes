Carbonara recipe




List of ingredients

You will need:

  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 40gr of Permesan Cheese
  • 1 clove of chopped garlic
  • 1 soup spoon of olive oil
  • 100gr of bacon
  • 200gr of spaghetti
  • Black pepper as you like

Preparation:

  1. Take the egg yolks with the parmesan cheese and mix it together in a bowl until it looks like a thick paste.
  2. Take the garlic clove, olive oil and the bacon in a pan at medium heat around 5 minutes. Start to bake the spaghetti at the same time.
  3. When the pasta is at al dente consistency turn of the heat take out the past to a bowl and save some cooking water from the pasta.
  4. Add the spaguetti to the pan, add the egg yolk mix and add some cooking water.
  5. Add some black pepper and serve with some basil.
